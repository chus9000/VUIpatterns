#3. Items List

Pattern 3. Items List
When listing items you have to choose from reading all the available ones to chunk them even one by one.
Some common options are:
- 1 item 🙋🏾
- 2 to 4 items + see more 👩‍👦-👩‍👩‍👧‍👧
- 5 items 👩‍👦👨‍👧‍👧
- Full list: 6 items or more 👨‍👨‍👧‍👧👩‍👩‍👦‍👦
